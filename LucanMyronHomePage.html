About the Job:

Job Description

Software Developer is sort of a blanket term. The duties of a developer can range from creating software and applications, designing machine code, developing video games, updating existing software, and so much more. A good software developer is knowledgeable in a few different coding languages. While they don't typically need to be an expert it is preferred that developers, new and seasoned, be familiar with concepts like software development life cycles (SDLCs), Object Oriented Programming, testing, and more.


Required Skills

Soft skills such as communication, networking, or teamwork will help applicants get their foot in the door. However, there is a wide range of skills that developers need to be more desirable. This includes certifications and knowledge in several different coding languages. JavaScript, Java, and Python are some of the most popular languages used in business today. Knowing what industry uses what and then learning that language(s) will prepare individuals best.


Required Training

When it comes to software development and many other developer jobs there are a myriad of avenues individuals can take. Coding boot camps, computer science degrees, and learning at your own pace are viable options. However, it is important to remember that that the developer field is heavily saturated and people applying for jobs will likely face a very rigorous interview process where their advanced skills and knowledge will be tested and likely ranked amongst 100's of other applicants. While a computer science degree isn't necessary, it should be considered. If that is not possible though, focusing on projects and gaining more and more experience with other coding languages will almost certainly give applicants a leg up.


Expected Salary

According to the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics the median annual pay is $132,270 for a software developer. With the lower end being over $77k and the higher range of over $208k. What this largely comes down to is location and type of job. There is also a person's self worth. In other words, don't be afraid to ask for more if you feel your talents have greater value.
